Progress — linear + circular

Two variants doing different jobs. Linear = "how much of a known total is done" (green, determinate). Circular = "something is happening" (dark, determinate or indeterminate, optionally stoppable). The colour split is intentional — green for "value accumulating", dark for "task in progress, neutral status."

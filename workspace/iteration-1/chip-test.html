Chip — all 10 cells × 2 types · Chip Group

Two types (Filter, Input) × five states (default, hover, selected, selected_hover, disabled) = 10 cells per type. Hover and selected-hover are force-rendered with .is-hover; the first cell in each row uses real :hover so you can verify natural state matches the forced state next to it. The Selected fill uses brand blue (#1047A1) — not Figma's near-black #16191C. Disabled cells don't pick up hover effects (guarded by :not([disabled]):not(.is-disabled)).
